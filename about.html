About Us


Holly & Ruby Cat Sitting Services are a network of sitters based in and around the Gwent area. The business started 10 years ago after a need was identified in the local area to offer an alternative to catteries for when pet owners are away from home. The demand for the service has increased year on year in the last decade, and we recently celebrated our 10 year anniversary with some cat themed goodies for our loyal customers!


What matters to us is that your cat is able to stay in a familiar environment whilst you are away, with as little stress and upheaval as possible. Individual needs of animals are taken into account and we tailor care to suit their needs. We are a group of cat lovers, passionate about what we do and the care we provide.


We regularly take part in fundraising activities for local animal welfare services, including RSPCA Wales and South Wales Cat Rescue. We also collect any unwanted food and pet supplies which can be donated to local services, so please get in touch if you have food, blankets or toys that are suitable for cats or dogs.

Benefits


There are many benefits to using a cat sitting service whilst you are away, as opposed to a traditional cattery. We take care of your pet at home, in their familiar environment. This prevents exposure to additional stress or potential health hazards/disease which may come with putting them into a cattery. We can also visit on a schedule that suits you and your cat, ensuring that your petâ€™s routine is not disturbed. We will feed your cat according to your instructions, and can also administer any medication if and when required. All our staff are fully criminal record checked and insured. In addition to feeding and medicating we will also empty and clean litter trays, and spend time playing with and cuddling your cat if they so desire! For nervous cats we will give them space and try to cause as little disruption as possible during visits. For peace of mind we also have access to 24hr emergency veterinary services, should your cat fall ill whilst in our care.


To arrange an introductory meeting, where we will meet you and your pet in your home to discuss your requirements, please get in touch via the Contact page.
